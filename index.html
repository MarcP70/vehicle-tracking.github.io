Features

Full control over your information.

At any time, you can create, modify, or delete information related to your vehicles, maintenance records, or your user account.

One-click vehicle search.

The automated search module allows you to retrieve your vehicle's specifications based on its registration.

Day/Night Mode

This mode provides a visually pleasing experience that is less tiring for the eyes, especially in low-light conditions.

About Vehicle Tracking

As a student at Hoberthon School, I learned the fundamentals of computer development during my first year, with a focus on both back-end and front-end web development. To showcase the acquired skills, there's nothing better than creating a personal portfolio. The idea of developing Vehicle Tracking naturally occurred to me. While personally maintaining my vehicles, I used to jot down follow-ups on paper. However, the need to record, organize, and archive these notes properly consumed a significant amount of time, with the constant risk of losing information. To address this issue, I created a web application that ensures data preservation, coupled with its user-friendly simplicity.
